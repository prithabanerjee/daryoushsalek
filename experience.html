MUSICAL EXPERIENCE

  • Teacher of Piano Performance at Warwick Independant School: Teaching solo piano, accompaniment, chamber music, conducting aural classes and music theory of all levels.

  • Performed in solo piano recitals and chamber ensembles both nationally and internationally including appearances at Birmingham Conservatoire’s Adrian Boult Hall and Recital Hall, and at the RNCM’s Carol Nash Recital Hall and Concert Hall.

  • Undertaken advanced pedagogy courses in piano at the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire and the Royal Northern College of Music.

  • Taught piano privately for ten years to both children and adults ranging from beginners to advanced level.

  • Have worked with a wide range of instrumentalists and singers in both accompaniment and chamber music.

  • Undertook the ‘Pianist for Dance’ scheme as a student with the Birmingham Royal Ballet.

  • Studied Harpsichord and Fortepiano at Birmingham Conservatoire with internationally renowned pianist David Ward.

  • Extensive experience working as a repetiteur with singers for musical theatre productions.
